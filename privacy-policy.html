Privacy Policy

Last updated:

At Nudge, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information that we receive through our Google Chrome extension.

1. Information We Collect

Nudge is designed to work locally within your browser. We do not harvest, read, or sell your emails or personal messages.

  • Local Data: To set reminders, the extension stores basic metadata locally in your browser (e.g., thread URLs, designated follow-up times, and basic notification context).
  • Sync Data: If you are signed into Chrome sync, Chrome syncs your settings and reminders across your devices according to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Analytics: We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage data (such as how many reminders are created daily) to improve extension performance, but this is never tied to personally identifiable information.

2. How We Use Information

We use the locally stored data exclusively to provide the core functionality of the extension—reminding you to follow up on specific conversations at the times you designate. We do not use your information for advertising or lead generation.

3. Third-Party Access

Nudge interacts with third-party websites (like Gmail, LinkedIn, and WhatsApp) strictly to generate the direct links to your conversations. We do not transmit your data to external servers, nor do we sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

Because your reminder data is stored locally in your browser storage or via Chrome Sync, it is secured by Google's native browser security mechanisms. We implement best practices in our extension permissions, ensuring we only request access to the specific URLs required to provide our service.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date of this Privacy Policy and, depending on the significance of the changes, we may provide a more prominent notice.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us at support@nudge-extension.com.
